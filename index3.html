Ladakh

Explore about Ladakh


Tourism in Ladakh is an absolute delight for nature lovers as well as adventurers. Adorned with the lofty barren mountains, Ladakh is home to some of the highest motorable passes like Khardung La (5359 m), Marsimik La (5582 m), and Chang La (5360 m), and alpine lakes like Pangong Tso (4350 m) and Tso Moriri (4522 m).

Ladakh is further divided into three regions - Leh, Nubra, and Zanskar, and each region has several places to see. Ladakh is majorly popular amongst the adventurers who feel welcomed by the number of adrenaline rushing activities offered here.

Famous Foods


Momos

It is a dumpling filled with meat or vegetable wrapped in dough.


Thukpa

It is a soupy noodle dish with assorted vegetables (for vegetarians) and minced meat (for non-vegetarians)


Skyu

The dish contains vegetable and dough kneaded into flattened thumb-sized balls.


Chhutagi

The flattened dough is usually cut into circular shapes and then given the shape of a bow-tie


Tingmo

Tingmo is a delicate, soft and fluffy bun that is prepared from wheat flour without putting any filling
